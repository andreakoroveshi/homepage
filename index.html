Bio

My name is Andrea, and I am an illustrator from Korçë, Albania. I like to paint landscapes, and occasionally people or portraits. Most recently, I have successfully helped in designing and developing the world of Stonesaga: a cooperative, campaign style, survival crafting board game by OOMM Games.

Experience

    3. Painted the board tiles of the game by combining 2D and 3D in my workflow.
    4. Painted hundreds of item art to be used in the item cards of the game, changing my art style to follow the directions of the art director.
